VOC­ALS

Vocals are all the human voice elements of your song, whether that be lyrical, melodic, chorale, etc. Much of the quality of vocals comes from the performance and recording, but there is still plenty of post-processing and production treatment you can do to achieve vocals that balance clarity, tone, and consistency.

A basic vocal processing chain usually includes: DeEsser > Pitch Corrector > Compressor > EQ > Reverb > Saturation > and then creative effects like Delay, Distortion, or Modulation.
