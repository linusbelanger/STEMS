PERCUS­SION

Percussion is the main rhythmic force of the song. While not always necessary, percussion, or, more commonly, drums, can help define timing, structure, and groove. Percussion comes in the form of any instrument that you hit, shake, or scrape, and is usually pitchless.

Like pitched instruments, percussion can be recorded, or virtually created with VSTs and samples.

TOOLS

A seemingly infinite community-driven library of free drum kits, loops, and samples, in the form of a subreddit.
