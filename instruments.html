TRACKS:

INSTRU­MENTS

Instrument tracks include everything from guitar to piano and synth. These tracks make up the main musical body of the song, providing things like chords, root notes, melodies, and solos. They can be real recorded instruments or virtual VST instruments.

Free VST instruments include Spitfire LABS, Vital Wavetable Synth, Addictive Keys, DecentSampler, and many more.

TOOLS

Below is a collection of free instrument tools that I personally use on a regular basis.


SPITFIRE LABS

An ever-expanding library of professionally recorded VST instruments.

 VITAL

A virtual spectral warping wavetable synthesizer. Preset libraries like Preset Share can help craft sounds.

 GUITAR RIG 7

A virtual guitar amp and multi-effects rack for recording DI (direct input) electric guitar.
LOOPERMAN

A community-driven library of royalty-free loops and samples with powerful search and filtering functions.

 SOUND ON SOUND

A music production and audio recording magazine / blog / forum.

 JSFXR

An online 8-bit sound generator for making retro sound effects in JavaScript.
